Correlations discovered between Age, Health risks, Income

With the increase of age the trend is towards decrease in healthcare and obesity. Smoking is shown to be on a positive correlation as age advances and reduced with the lower income range. Note that the age of the participants of this study range between 30 and 45. With this short range there may not be enough evidence to show a strong correlation in these comparisons. The trends mentioned here cannot be underlined as further studies would need to be conducted to emphasize this analysis with a wide range of age groups to support these findings.
